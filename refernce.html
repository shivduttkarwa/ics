Jesuit tradition
for today's world

Riverview offers a holistic Catholic education for boys, one that nurtures their faith and encourages lifelong growth. Guided by the spirit of Saint Ignatius of Loyola and grounded in values, we strive to form young men who are curious, thoughtful and ready to contribute meaningfully to an ever-changing world.

Hear from our Principal
Hear from our Rector

Conscience

Conscience

Speaking truthfully.

Showing a commitment to deep reflection and contemplation.

Seeking, questioning and exploring faith and so developing an understanding and practice for today’s world.

Showing the ability to discern the rightness and goodness of their own choices; an understanding of the importance of responsibility.

Showing a deep gratitude toward others through words and actions.

Competence

Competence

Showing a willingness and commitment to learning in and beyond the classroom.

Demonstrating persistence in and commitment to the pursuit of excellence – the development of skills and abilities to contribute to fulfilment.

Showing curiosity and asking questions to understand and resolve problems so that they become learners for life.

Showing the capacity to work and flourish with others; to show willingness and commitment to collaborating and developing with others.

Showing an unyielding spirit in the pursuit of excellence in character.

Compassion

Compassion

Being respectful toward others; and nurturing respectful relationships with staff and peers.

Seeking opportunities to help and support others; showing generosity of spirit in words and actions.

Responding to differences with compassion and empathy.

Showing feelings of charity and compassion and a sense of justice for and solidarity with others.

Commitment

Commitment

Showing persistence in achieving aspirations; the commitment to goals and intentions in developing my unique gifts and skills.

Showing a leadership spirit – self-awareness, courage and compassion in action.

Showing a commitment to valuing the dignity and worth of each of the members of our community.

Showing Care for our Campus – the care and concern for people and place.

Shows a desire to be an agent of change.

A Journey of Growth

Learning

We believe education is more than just academic excellence. From the moment a student joins our College, he is welcomed into a community that cares deeply for his growth — intellectually, spiritually and personally.

Faith and Service

Faith is central to Jesuit education, and students are challenged to become fully who they were created to be—to think deeply, act with integrity and serve others.

Boarding

Boarding offers boys a second home filled with care, structure and friendship. They learn independence, responsibility and confidence while living in a community that supports their growth every step of the way.

Co-Curriculum

Our extensive Co-Curriculum Program opens doors for students to discover new interests, develop their skills, and strengthen their character through grit, teamwork, and grace.

Ignatian Formation

Our Jesuit tradition calls us to enter into solidarity with the poor, the marginalised, and the voiceless, to enable their participation in the processes that shape the society in which we all live. They, in turn, teach us about our own poverty as no document can.

Our Campus

Established in 1880, the College is committed to an education for each student that proposes Christ as the model for human life by promoting the spiritual, academic, social, physical, and experiential growth of its students.

Our unique Programs

Magis and Ignatian Service Program

Students learn to look outward, act with purpose and serve where they are needed most. Through reflection and real service, they discover how small actions can bring meaningful change to others.

Explore the Program

Our unique Programs

Caring for Our Common Home

Boys explore their responsibility to the world around them, learning stewardship through hands-on projects and daily choices. They grow in awareness, gratitude and a genuine desire to protect our shared environment.

Explore the Program

Our unique Programs

First Nations Program

Students engage in respectful learning, listening and partnership with First Nations communities. They gain deeper understanding, build real connections and learn what it means to walk together with humility and openness.

Explore the Program

Our unique Programs

Immersions

Immersions take boys beyond the familiar, inviting them into communities where empathy grows and perspectives shift. These experiences help them understand the world more deeply and inspire a lasting commitment to service.
